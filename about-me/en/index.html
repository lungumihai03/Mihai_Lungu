About me

My name is Mihai Lungu and I am a student at Ion Creangă Pedagogical University in Chișinău, Moldova. I am studying Computer Science for my Bachelor's degree, and I enjoy teaching myself various programming languages such as C#, C++, C, Python, Visual Basic, Visual FoxPro, and Java. In my free time, I like to read and play video games. I am a sentimental person and I enjoy contemplating the future. In the near future, I have big plans to establish my own IT company. I aspire to create an environment conducive to innovation and technology, where people can work together and bring innovative solutions to the ever-evolving digital world. I am excited about the prospects that this field brings and I am determined to build my career in the technology industry.
