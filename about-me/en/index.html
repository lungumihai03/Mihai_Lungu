About me:

As a recent graduate in Computer Science from the Faculty of Physics, Mathematics and Information Technologies at the "Ion Creangă" State Pedagogical University of Chișinău, I am driven by a strong passion for programming and the transformative power of technology. My academic journey has solidified my foundation in software development, and now, as I prepare to continue my studies at the master's level in the same field, I am more motivated than ever to deepen my knowledge and specialize in areas that truly inspire me.

I enjoy tackling complex problems, building functional and elegant solutions, and continuously challenging myself to learn new technologies and approaches. Whether it's developing desktop applications in C#, experimenting with cryptography, or exploring web development and AI concepts, I thrive on creativity, logic, and continuous improvement.

My goal is to become a skilled and versatile software developer, contribute to impactful projects, and remain a lifelong learner. I’m currently seeking opportunities to get involved in real-world projects, research, or collaborations that will help shape and refine my career path in the tech world.
